NFCI index - Chicago Fed

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/NFCI
Update Frequency: Weekly - updated every Wednesday at 8:30 a.m. ET, covering data through the previous Friday. If a federal holiday falls on Wednesday or earlier in the week, it's updated on Thursday instead. Chicagofed


Initial Claims for Unemployment - BLS

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/ICSA
Update Frequency: Weekly - typically updated every Thursday with data from the week ending the previous Saturday. The Employment and Training Administration (ETA) publishes this data from the Unemployment Insurance Weekly Claims Report.


Advanced Retail Sales - Commerce Dept / Census

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/RSAFS
Update Frequency: Monthly - the Advance Monthly Retail Sales reports are released about 9 working days after the close of the reference month. These provide advance estimates for the reporting month and preliminary sales data for the previous month. Census


Inflation CPI

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CPIAUCSL
Update Frequency: Monthly - released around the middle of each month with data for the previous month. For example, the CPI release for March 2025 is scheduled for April 10, 2025, at 8:30 a.m. ET. Bls


2yr Treasuries

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DGS2
Update Frequency: Daily - updated every business day when markets are open, reflecting the market yield on 2-year Treasury securities.


10yr Treasuries

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DGS10
Update Frequency: Daily - updated every business day when markets are open, reflecting the market yield on 10-year Treasury securities.


Yield curve - 10yr-2yr spread

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/T10Y2Y
Update Frequency: Daily - calculated as the difference between the 10-year and 2-year Treasury constant maturity rates, updated every business day.


Housing Prices

URL: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CSUSHPINSA
Update Frequency: Monthly - the S&P/Case-Shiller U.S. National Home Price Index is released with a lag, typically on the last Tuesday of each month with data from two months prior.


MEASUREMENT:CSUSHPINSA
title: S&P/Case-Shiller U.S. National Home Price Index
description: Measures changes in the value of the residential real estate market in the United States
frequency: monthly
units: Index Jan 2000=100

MEASUREMENT:DGS10
title: 10-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Rate
description: Interest rate on U.S. Treasury securities at 10-year constant maturity
frequency: daily
units: %

MEASUREMENT:DGS2
title: 2-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Rate
description: Interest rate on U.S. Treasury securities at 2-year constant maturity
frequency: daily
units: %

MEASUREMENT:T10Y2Y
title: 10-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Minus 2-Year Treasury Constant Maturity
description: Difference between the 10-year and 2-year Treasury yields, a common recession predictor
frequency: daily
units: %

MEASUREMENT:CPIAUCSL
title: Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers: All Items
description: Measures the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services
frequency: monthly
units: Index 1982-1984=100

MEASUREMENT:RSAFS
title: Advance Retail Sales: Retail and Food Services, Total
description: Total sales of retail and food services that are adjusted for seasonal variations
frequency: monthly
units: Millions of Dollars

MEASUREMENT:ICSA
title: Initial Claims for Unemployment Insurance
description: Number of people filing new claims for unemployment insurance benefits
frequency: weekly
units: Number of Claims

MEASUREMENT:NFCI
title: Chicago Fed National Financial Conditions Index
description: Provides a comprehensive update on U.S. financial conditions in money markets, debt and equity markets, and the traditional and shadow banking systems
frequency: weekly
units: Index
